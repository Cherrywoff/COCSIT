About COCSIT Latur

🔭 Vision Statement

To impart quality education and job-oriented training in the fields of Computer Science, Information Technology, Biotechnology, and Management Science.

🎯 Mission Statement

To provide the best possible education by fostering innovation, critical thinking, and practical coding skills designed for industry recruitment.

📜 Institutional Motto

Governing Society

Royal Education Society, Latur

Established in 2001 by Dr. M. R. Patil, the Royal Education Society was founded with a dream to spread professional computer training to students in the rural areas of Marathwada, Maharashtra. Today, the society manages top institutes equipped with modern research wings, computer labs, and biotechnology divisions.

🎓 Teaching Methodologies

We follow modern guidelines aligned with NEP 2020, focusing on practical programming exercises, internal credit projects, and seminar sessions.

📢 Attendance Rules

75% lecture attendance is strictly mandatory to sit for autonomous terminal examinations. Medical leaves must be certified by registered officers.
